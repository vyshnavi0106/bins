Making of Machuria

Ingredients required

  • cornflour
  • cabbage
  • carrot
  • chilli paste
  • plain flour
  • pepper powder
  • salt
  • oil
  • soya sauce
  • chilli sauce
  • venigar
  • water

steps to prepare

  1. boil the veggies for 3 to 4 mins.
  2. add the veggies,corn flour,pepper,salt to a mixing bowl.Mix every thing well.
  3. Mix everything and make lemon sized balls.The balls must bind without breaking.
  4. heat the oil in a deep pan for deep frying.
  5. gently slide the balls in hot oil & fry on a medium flame.
  6. donot disturb them for 1 min after the balls are dropped in oil.
  7. After few seconds of frying, you can keep stirring and fry until they turn golden.
  8. now take the balls out from the pan.
  9. For making of manchurian sauce ,heat oil in a pan,then add ginger garlic paste and saute for a min.
  10. add soya sauce,chilly paste,red chilly sauce,vinegar and some water in the pan.saute them for 1 to 2 min.
  11. Now add the prepared manchurian balls into the sauce pan.
  12. serve the manchurian with fried rice or noodles.
